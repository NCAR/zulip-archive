Topic: NASA Earth Sciences & UW Hackweek

Katie Dagon (Apr 23 2024 at 16:54):

The eScience Institute at the University of Washington is inviting applications for their annual hackweek program Aug 19-23rd in Seattle, WA. Hackweeks are designed to support academic students and researchers at the graduate level and higher, as well as data scientists, engineers, technicians, and industry partners. UW Hackweeks have always been about collaboration, learning, and fun. All tutorials and project work will assume participants are familiar with Python-based computing in the geosciences as described at Pythia Foundations.

Learn more and apply by 11:59pm PT April 30th at event.hackweek.io.
