Topic: ESDS Forum: Open Discussion on NCAR's JupyterHub Service

Please join the Earth System Data Sciences (ESDS) community on March 7th at 2pm Mountain Time for an open discussion on the use of JupyterHub for interactive analysis computations on NCAR’s NWSC computing resources. A short overview of JupyterHub will be provided, followed by open discussion.

Consider joining the conversation if you want to learn more or have thoughts about how this valuable resource should continue to evolve to better meet user needs.
