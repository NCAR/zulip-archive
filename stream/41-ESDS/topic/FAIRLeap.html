Another relevant event:

We are pleased to announce FAIRLeap, an upcoming virtual workshop hosted by the LinkedEarth FROGS initiative. This workshop is designed to enhance the dissemination of geoscience research according to the FAIR principles—ensuring that all research outputs are Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable.

Workshop Dates: February 11-14, 2025
Registration Deadline: January 31, 2025

Participants will gain proficiency in essential tools for open science publishing such as GitHub, Docker, and myBinder, and will receive guidance on preparing manuscripts for publication under FAIR standards. The workshop will cater to those aiming to elevate the impact and reproducibility of their research.

For details and registration information, please visit linked.earth/FROGS/. We look forward to your participation.
The LinkedEarth team
