We've just posted a Data Driven Workflows Analyst -Associate Scientist II (AS-II) position in CISL that will focus on developing/improving data driven workflows that leverage open source software components and will research the capabilities of the Open Science Data Federation (OSDF, https://osg-htc.org/services/osdf) to better support these workflows with distributed data and compute resources. Code examples developed by this position will be documented and published to community accessible platforms for reuse. The AS-II will also lead community hackathons to develop and exercise community reference workflows, focussed on climate impacts research, as part of their responsibility and will work closely with the ESDS community in doing so. Please apply or send this announcement along to others who may be interested.
