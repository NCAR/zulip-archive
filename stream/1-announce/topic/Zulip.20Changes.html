Nick Wehrheim (Mar 12 2024 at 18:19):

Starting tomorrow, our Zulip instance will no longer have unlimited free access to Zulip’s Mobile Push Notification Service. We are in continued communication with Zulip to address this and are also looking into other chat options due to the restructuring of Zulip's software plans and policies.
We are also aware of the broken search functionality since the last upgrade. We are trying to address it and unfortunately the broken capability may be outside and beyond any effort we are able to try. Please stay tuned and we appreciate your patience.

Katelyn FitzGerald (Apr 21 2025 at 22:05):

Hi @all ,

As I mentioned at the ESDS Forum last week we will be making a transition from this Zulip instance to a Slack workspace in the near future. We’ll be sharing more details and invitations soon, but in the meantime I wanted to share some information regarding timelines and archives and give folks the opportunity to raise any questions or concerns.

In terms of timelines, Zulip will continue to be available through May 16, 2025 at which point it will go read-only and be decommissioned shortly thereafter. Public streams will be archived and available here: https://ncar.github.io/zulip-archive/. However, data from private streams and direct messages will not be archived so we encourage you to transfer any information that might be needed from those as soon as possible.

You can expect invitations to the Slack workspace sometime late this week and we’ll be looking for feedback from folks regarding how to make that space work best for our community.

If you have questions or concerns you can reach out to me via DM or email.
