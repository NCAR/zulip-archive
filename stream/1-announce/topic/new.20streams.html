Matt Long (Oct 28 2020 at 15:24):

I just created #pinatubo-LE to coordinate development and planning of some new CESM1-LE integrations. We are planning to replicate a period of the CESM1-LE spanning the 1991 Mt Pinatubo eruption, censoring the volcanic aerosol forcing from this event. By comparing these runs with the full CESM-LE, we aim to quantify the effects the Mt. Pinatubo eruption explicitly. Our primary goals are related to ocean BGC, but there may be other interesting things to look at in these runs. Join the conversation if interested.
