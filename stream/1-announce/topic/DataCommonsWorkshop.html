Express your interest for “Innovations in Open Science (IOS) Planning Workshop: Community Expectations for a Geoscience Data Commons”

Please express your interest in attending this workshop, to be held May 29-31, 2024, in Boulder, CO.
This workshop will focus on developing community requirements to modernize community-accessible data science infrastructure, to better connect our geoscience datasets with geoscience-focused analytics environments, and to support researcher needs in meeting data-sharing expectations. The overall goal is to allow a broad and diverse user community to share, integrate, analyze, and visualize geoscience research data to drive scientific discovery.
For more information and to express your interest, visit the workshop web page.
