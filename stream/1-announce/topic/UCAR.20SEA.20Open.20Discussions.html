Topic: UCAR SEA Open Discussions

Katelyn FitzGerald (Mar 01 2024 at 17:31):

@Negin Sobhani and I are co-hosting a UCAR SEA Open Discussion on Documentation on March 14th (details below) open to interested NCAR/UCAR staff! Please join us if you're interested and feel free to reach out with questions.

What: SEA Open Discussion on Documentation
When: March 14 at 1pm (MT) - calendar event
How: Virtual Meeting Link
Who: interested NCAR/UCAR staff

Join the UCAR Software Engineering Assembly for a lightly-moderated discussion on software documentation across UCAR.

Any discussion topics are welcome, as are all experience levels. We encourage you to share your experiences, good and bad.

Some potential discussion topics include:

  • Documentation-related tooling (e.g. languages, documentation generators, hosting, etc.)
  • Types of documentation and frameworks for identifying documentation needs (e.g. Diátaxis)
  • Challenges and successes in adopting modern documentation frameworks
  • Examples worth emulating
  • Best practices and standards for documentation and perhaps good and good-enough practices as well
  • What could SEA do to help support and/or advocate for community needs?
