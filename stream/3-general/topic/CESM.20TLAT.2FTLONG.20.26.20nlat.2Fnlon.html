@Holly Olivarez, the standard 1° POP grid has a displaced north pole (it's over Greenland). nlat and nlon are simply index variables corresponding to the dimension of the grid arrays (i.e., TLAT, TLONG, etc.). More here:
https://pop-tools.readthedocs.io/en/latest/examples/get-model-grid.html
